What is attest.ink?

A decentralized protocol for creating cryptographic proof that AI helped create your content. Everything runs directly in your browser using mathematical signatures to ensure authenticity. Your content never leaves your device, and verification happens independently without relying on any central authority or database.

Why it's decentralized: Attestations are self-contained cryptographic proofs that can be verified by anyone, anywhere, without connecting to our servers. The protocol is open source, works offline, and doesn't require blockchain transactions - making it truly peer-to-peer and censorship-resistant.

How It Works

1. Select Your AI Model

Choose from a wide range of AI models including:

  • GPT-4 Series (OpenAI)
  • Claude Opus 4 (Anthropic)
  • Gemini Pro (Google)
  • Llama 3 (Meta)
  • And many more...

2. Create Your Attestation

Input your content and generate a cryptographic attestation:

{
  "content_hash": "sha256:b6a5c8d9...",
  "model": "gpt-4",
  "timestamp": "2025-01-15T12:00:00Z"
}

3. Add Digital Signature

Sign with your Ethereum wallet or create a local signature for cryptographic proof.

4. Display Verification Badge

Add verification badges to your content. Anyone can click to verify authenticity.
