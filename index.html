Transparent attribution for the AI era

Clear distinction between human and AI-generated content

In an era where content origin matters more than ever, attest.ink provides the ability to distinguish human created content from content generated with AI assistance.

Simple Implementation

Add these lightweight badges to any digital content with just a few lines of code.

<!-- For human-generated content -->
<img src="https://yourusername.github.io/attest.ink/assets/badges/human-generated.svg" alt="Human Generated" width="120" height="30">

<!-- For AI-generated content -->
<img src="https://yourusername.github.io/attest.ink/assets/badges/ai-generated.svg" alt="AI Generated" width="120" height="30">
![Human Generated](https://yourusername.github.io/attest.ink/assets/badges/human-generated.svg)
![AI Generated](https://yourusername.github.io/attest.ink/assets/badges/ai-generated.svg)
<script src="https://yourusername.github.io/attest.ink/js/attest.js"></script>
<script>
  // Add a human-generated badge to an element
  AttestInk.addBadge('human', '#my-human-content');
  
  // Add an AI-generated badge to an element
  AttestInk.addBadge('ai', '#my-ai-content');
</script>
